What is Clixel?

We are a website that allows users the chance to win money without spending money!

The concept is simple - there is a hidden pixel somewhere on the sponsor board. Clicking it will load a message from the respective sponsor. After the message plays, it is revealed whether you won the money. If not, the pixel changes locations and you can click the board again.

Clixel allows users the opportunity to win money in their spare time. Bored on the bus? Log in and click some pixels! In a boring meeting? Pull out your phone and click some pixels!

It's really that simple. So start clicking!

Requirements to Participate

No purchase necessary! In order to win, you must create an account. To create an account, you must follow the laws in your area. If it is illegal for you to participate in our website, DO NOT create an account. Your account will become void if it is discovered that any laws have been violated.
