THE AUCTION HOUSE

What is the auction house?

After puchasing space on our ad board, you have the ability to sell that space. You can sell it directly through our site or have it go through our auction house.

Why would I sell my ad space?

As the available ad space on our board decreases, the demand will increase. This will naturally increase the resale value of everyone's ad space. While some people will choose to keep their ad space, others will choose to resell it and make a quick buck.
